# Cultivation Tracker: Habit Check-in

> A SiYuan Note plugin inspired by "A Record of a Mortal's Journey to Immortality", helping you build daily habits through gamification.

---

## The Legend

In the chaotic void, there lived a mortal named **Han Li**. With mediocre talent — a four-element false spiritual root — he was destined never to walk the path of immortality.

But fate is unpredictable. Through a stroke of serendipity, Han Li obtained a mysterious small green bottle capable of maturing spiritual herbs. From then on, with his mortal body, he embarked on the long journey of cultivation.

Qi Condensation, Foundation Establishment, Core Formation, Nascent Soul, Soul Formation, Void Refinement, Body Integration, Mahayana... Each breakthrough required day after day of diligent cultivation and accumulation.

Han Li often reflected: "Cultivators value perseverance above all. Without unwavering will, even with heaven-defying treasures, one cannot achieve the Great Dao."

## The Way of Cultivation

The Great Dao has three thousand paths, all leading to the same destination.

Modern people also have their way of cultivation — **building habits, accumulating day by day**.

- Daily reading accumulates spiritual stones to open wisdom
- Daily exercise strengthens the body to build the foundation
- Daily learning refines skills to form the golden core
- Daily reflection examines oneself to refine the nascent soul

Yet mortal will is weak. Three days fishing, two days drying nets — the path of cultivation often ends in failure.

## A Treasure for the Ages

Fortunately, SiYuan Notes exists as a modern cultivator's **cave dwelling**.

This plugin, modeled after the cultivation system, transforms habit-building into a cultivation journey:

- **Spirit Stones**: Earned from daily check-ins, recording your cultivation progress
- **Realms**: Qi Condensation, Foundation Establishment, Core Formation... step by step toward the Dao
- **Wish List**: Make wishes, redeem with spirit stones, motivate yourself to keep cultivating
- **Cultivation Calendar**: Observe your actions, know your path, see your cultivation trajectory at a glance
- **Achievement Badges**: Milestones on the long cultivation road, recording each breakthrough

## Begin Your Cultivation

- Click the "Cultivation Tracker" icon in the sidebar
- Click the spirit stone buttons to add today's cultivation points
- Create wishes and redeem them with accumulated spirit stones
- View your cultivation calendar and achievement badges to track your progress
